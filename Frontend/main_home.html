How to Book an Appointment

Follow these simple steps to book your appointment with the Municipal Commissioner

1

Register

Create a new citizen account with your mobile number

2

Login

Login to your citizen account using mobile number & password

3

Book Appointment

Fill in the booking form with group details and take a live photo

4

Wait for Confirmation

PA reviews your request and sends a confirmation or reschedule notice

Tip: Groups of multiple persons can be booked by a single leader. Only the leader's Aadhaar is required. Bring all member names and arrive 15 minutes early.
