About Amravati Municipal Corporation

Serving the citizens of Amravati with dedication and digital governance

History & Establishment

The Amravati Municipal Corporation (AMC) is the governing body of the city of Amravati in Maharashtra, India. It was officially established in 1983, replacing the earlier municipal council to manage the growing urban needs of the city.

AMC oversees civic infrastructure, public health, sanitation, water supply, waste management, road development, and various welfare services for the citizens.

Mission

Our mission is to ensure efficient administration, modern infrastructure, citizen-friendly governance, and sustainable urban development in Amravati.

Key Functions

  • Maintaining roads, drainage, and public facilities
  • Providing safe drinking water and sanitation
  • Waste management & cleanliness drives
  • Urban planning & development projects
  • Public health and welfare services
