Our Services

Delivering essential services for the citizens of Amravati

Water Supply

Providing clean and safe drinking water across Amravati with efficient distribution and monitoring.

Waste Management

Door-to-door garbage collection, disposal, and cleanliness drives to maintain hygiene in the city.

Sanitation

Ensuring clean streets, public toilets, and proper drainage systems for a healthier environment.

Roads & Infrastructure

Maintenance of roads, streetlights, and urban infrastructure to support safe and smooth travel.

Public Health

Health awareness programs, vaccination drives, and medical camps for citizen welfare.

Welfare Programs

Various schemes for education, housing, and development of underprivileged citizens.
